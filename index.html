Short Biography

I received the B. E. degree and M. E. degree in Control Engineering from Hohai University and Wuhan University, China, in 2012 and 2015, respectively. I obtained my Ph.D. agreem from University of Groningen in December 2019. Since January, 2020, I have been a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Riverside.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Modeling of Brain Networks

  • Synchronization

  • Nonlinear systems

  • Stochastic systems

  • Social Networks
