Short Biography

  • Since Jan. 2020, I have been a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Riverside, advised by Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti.

  • In Dec. 2019, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from University of Groningen. My advisor was Prof. Ming Cao.

  • Before that, I did my master in Wuhan University, China.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Analysis and control in brain networks

  • Reinforcement Learning with theoretical guarantee

  • Synchronization in oscillator networks

  • Stochastic systems
